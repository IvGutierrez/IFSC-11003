Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

Progressive Enhancementis the desgin process of focusing on the content/HTML first, then CSS, and then finally JavaScript It's important since follow this desgin philoshipy forces desginers to plan a rich and stable core instead of messing around with new tech that leaves people with old devices a website that barely works. I feel like the Video Game industry could follow this philosophy, but instead they large developers such as Activision make unoptimized games that take over half your hard drive, or run badly on everything but the top 1% of computers.

Another thing that progressive enchancement is useful for is internet connections like this article mentions. Due to my job, sometimes I end up in somewhat remote locations or in buildings that apparently build their roof with lead, which leads to dodgy internet connection, which means apps and webpages loaded with an overly desgined page take forever to load.
